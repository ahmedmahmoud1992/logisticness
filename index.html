About Us


Logisticness is an innovative freight forwarding company founded in 2024 by a team of industry veterans.
We're on a mission to revolutionize global logistics through exceptional service, cutting-edge technology, and a collaborative, customer-centric approach.

Our Services


Sea Freight

Streamlined transoceanic trade: Experience hassle-free shipping for your 20ft and 40ft container goods, facilitating your import and export operations across the globe.

Air Freight

Global air import and export solutions: Optimize your supply chain with our comprehensive air freight capabilities, providing rapid and dependable movement of your shipments across borders.

Custom Clearance

Expedite Your Cross-Border Logistics: Trust our technical brokerage expertise to handle customs clearance, eliminating delays and facilitating the efficient movement of your goods across borders.

in-land transport

Nationwide Egyptian Port Connectivity: Expand the reach of your import and export operations with our extensive inland transportation network, providing door-to-door services from and to all major Egyptian ports.

warehousing

Take advantage of our reliable warehousing services to keep your shipments protected from any unforeseen circumstances, allowing you to focus on your core business activities with peace of mind.

consulting

Comprehensive Shipping Guidance from Logistics Professionals: Our team of seasoned logistics experts will be with you every step of the way, offering tailored advice on the most suitable shipping routes and container options to meet the unique requirements of your cargo.
